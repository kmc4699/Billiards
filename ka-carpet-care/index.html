FAQ

Common questions.

How long do carpets take to dry?

Drying time depends on carpet type, weather, ventilation and how heavily the carpet was soiled. We provide an estimate after inspection.

Do I need to move furniture?

Please remove small and fragile items. Larger furniture can be discussed when quoting.

Can every stain be removed?

Many stains can be improved, but permanent dye damage, bleaching or old set-in stains may not disappear completely.

Do you clean rental properties?

Yes. Move-out carpet cleaning is available for tenants, landlords and property managers.
